About:

34th Street Bar & Grill, one of a few premier Brandon restaurants, is found in the Victoria Inn Hotel and Convention Centre. Enjoy casual family dining with a contemporary atmosphere for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Our menus are filled with a variety of delicious meals guaranteed to please.  Unique to other Brandon restaurants, is our partnership with Certified Angus Beef® Brand. Delicious quality steaks are our specialty, but we also offer gluten free pizza, seafood and numerous chicken selections.

Choose a romantic booth off in the corner or enjoy the natural light as it filters in on our patio by the pool. Take advantage of private In-room dining.

Tempt Your Taste Buds and Try One of Everything

Few Brandon restaurants offer Sunday brunch. Ours is held weekly from 10am until 2pm featuring an array of mouth watering items sure to please everyone in the family.

Nightly specials are always offered in the lounge. Enjoy a beverage in the bar while you and your friends gather to watch Jets TV and nibble on appetizers.
